Description

Snakes and Ladders is very old and addictive game. You can play this game with everyone. This is multiplayer game. You can also play snake and ladder game with computer and your friends.

Snake and Ladder board is very big and it covers full screen. You will really enjoy playing snake and ladder on big board. There are 10 snakes which will make game very difficult and bring you downward but there are 5 ladders will help you to finish the game by moving upward.

A real time pass game with friends. You will love to play this snake and ladder game. This game is also called as sap sidi.

Features:

  • Play this game with multiple options like 2 players, 3 players and 4 players.
  • When you get ladder you will move upward.
  • When snake bites you will move downward.
  • Very attractive animated 3D dice.
  • Sound supported.
  • Beautiful and user friendly interface to play.
  • Very good player moving and dice rolling animation.
