Description

Dominoes is one of the most popular and addictive board games in the world. This game offers you a great dominoes experience with stunning graphics, smooth gameplay, and multiple game modes.

Whether you are a beginner or a pro, you will find something to enjoy in this game. Download it now and join millions of players who love this classic game.

TIPS

- Dominoes Board Game comes with 5 game modes. To start a game, select the game mode, the number of players (2 to 4) and the score to win.

- The goal of the game is to get rid of all your tiles before your opponents do.

DOMINATE THE LEAGUES - Beat the other players and prove that you’re a true card shark when it comes to Texas Holdem poker.

- The player who has the largest double starts the game. After that, each player must place a tile on either end of the domino chain, matching the number of pips (dots) on the adjacent tile. For example, if there is a 4-2 tile on one end of the chain, you can place a 4-x or a 2-x tile next to it.

- The game ends when one player runs out of tiles or when no one can make a move.

- To adjust the game settings, such as sound effects, music, and animations, tap on the gear icon on the top left corner of the screen. You can also choose different backgrounds, avatars, and domino sets to customize your game experience.
